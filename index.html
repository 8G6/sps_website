Student Professional Awareness Summit

26th, 27th, & 28th November 2021

About

Student Professional Awareness Summit 2021 (SPAS) is a virtual global event organised by IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally in association with Signal Processing Society and Power and Energy Society . This event aims to inspire, educate, and empower students by conducting online workshops and session related to signal processing.

SPAS 2021 will be held from 26th to 28th November 2021

Students can now visit the Events tab to know more about the sessions and workshops.

Detailed guidelines of the event will be published on 25th November 2021.
